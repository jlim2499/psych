A new diet has been designed to prevent relapse in people with bipolar disorder. Two hundred volunteers with bipolar disorder are recruited for a study comparing this new diet with treatment as usual. The following results are obtained after 2 years.

Experienced relapseNo relapse
Special diet4060
Control diet9010


181.What is the absolute risk reduction for the special dietary approach?
50% ARR = CER - EER
= (90 / 100) - (40 / 100)
= 0.5 or 50%



182.What is the number needed to treat for the special dietary approach?
2 NNT = 1 / ARR
= 1 / 0.5
= 2



183.What is the relative risk reduction for the special dietary approach?
56% RRR = ARR / CER
= 0.5 / 0.9
= 0.56 (2.d.p)
