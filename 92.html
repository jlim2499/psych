To calculate the relative risk or odds ratio, data on outcomes needs to be of what type?

Dichotomous

Dichotomous outcomes (e.g. dead / alive) can be reported as proportions, percentages, risk, odds, risk ratios, odds ratios, number needed to treat, likelihood ratios, sensitivity, specificity, and pre-test and post-test probability.

The other data types cannot be reported in the same way as they are not binary.
