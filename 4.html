Which of the following is true regarding the GRADE approach used in evidence based medicine?

The system can be applied to observational studies

The GRADE system can be applied to observational studies.

An RCT can be down graded by the GRADE system if there are flaws. This might result in an RCT being considered to be very low in quality. Alternatively, an observational study may be promoted if it was conducted really well and produced very strong results.
