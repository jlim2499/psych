Which of the following is true regarding economic evaluation studies?

In a cost-effectiveness analysis no attempt is made to monetise benefits

In CEA no attempt is made to monetise benefits. This type of study is generally done when benefits cannot be monetised. In a CBA the benefits are monetised.

A dominated treatment is one that is both more expensive and less effective than some other treatment. It is never optimal to use a dominated treatment, because there is always a more effective and cheaper alternative available.
