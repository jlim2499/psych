CINAHL CINAHL is the nursing equivalent to MEDLINE.


59.An international researcher is keen to establish if there have been any randomised control trials comparing treatment in a general medical ward with treatment in a psychiatric ward
Cochrane Library The Cochrane library would quickly provide an answer to this question.
