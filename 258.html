Select the type of bias described in each of the following

316.A form of selection bias that may occur when hospital controls are used in a case control study
Berkson's bias Berkson's bias is known as hospital patient bias. It is not necessary to understand it in detail. Just remember it occurs in case control studies where hospital patients are used as controls.


317.A form of observer bias that occurs when subjects change their behaviour simply due to the fact that they are being observed.
Hawthorne effect Hawthorne bias occurs when a study incorrectly concludes that an intervention has led to a change in an outcome when in fact it is the observation itself which has resulted in a change.


318.A form of bias that can be demonstrated using a funnel plot
Publication bias Publication bias occurs when important evidence has failed to be considered. Funnel plots can be used to check for this.
