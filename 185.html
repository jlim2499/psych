Which of the following is best described as a nominal variable?

Ethnic group

Explanation for the Correct Answer:

Ethnic group is the correct option. A nominal variable is one that categorizes data without a natural order or ranking. Ethnic groups are categories that do not have a mathematical order or rank.

Explanation for the Incorrect Answers:

Height (in meters) is a ratio variable, as it can be measured on a continuous scale and has a true zero point.

Social class, although often considered a categorical variable, typically has an inherent order (lower, middle, upper) and is therefore ordinal rather than nominal.

Weight (is measured in grams) is also a ratio variable for the same reasons as height'it is continuous and has a true zero.

Age (if categorised as young, middle-aged, or old) represents an ordinal variable because the categories have a natural order.
