Which of the following is likely to increase the prevalence of a condition?

A new treatment for an aggressive form of cancer is discovered that reduces mortality

The new cancer treatment sounds great as less people will die. Unfortunately this will result in an increase in the prevalence of the condition.

Remember that:

Prevalence = incidence x duration of condition

Therefore the prevalence is increased by an increase in the incidence or duration of a condition. It is reduced by reductions in the incidence (decreased detection if diagnostic centres close or successful preventative measures are introduced) or by reductions in the duration of the condition.
