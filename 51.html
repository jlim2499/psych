A meta-analysis of 5 trials looking at the effect of depot olanzapine versus depot risperidone on psychotic symptoms (measured by the use of PANSS) finds a difference between the two in each trial. Which of the following trials produced a statistically significant result with a significance level set at 5%?

Trial 2 shows a reduction of 2 on the PANSS (p=0.001)

The question asks for the statistically significant result which relates to p values not the most clinically meaningful result which would relate to the PANSS score.

Results with p values less than 0.05 are said to be 'statistically significant'. This is based on the convention of an alpha level set at 5%.
