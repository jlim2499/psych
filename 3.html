Which of the following is a quality measure for the reporting of meta-analyses?

PRISMA

Previous Exam Question

Explanation for the Correct Answer:

PRISMA (Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses) is the correct option. PRISMA is an evidence-based minimum set of items for reporting in systematic reviews and meta-analyses. It focuses on ways in which authors can ensure the transparent and complete reporting of this type of research.
