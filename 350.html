The hypothesis that a researcher is trying to prove is called the?

Alternative hypothesis

Explanation for the Correct Answer:

Alternative hypothesis is the correct term. It is the hypothesis that a researcher is trying to prove or validate. In hypothesis testing, the alternative hypothesis proposes a new effect or relationship, and the test determines whether there is enough evidence to reject the null hypothesis in favor of the alternative hypothesis.

Explanation for the Incorrect Answers:

Null hypothesis is the hypothesis that there is no effect or no difference, and it is what the researcher aims to test against, not to prove.

Optional hypothesis is not a term commonly used in the context of hypothesis testing in research methodology.

Elective hypothesis is not a standard term in research or statistical hypothesis testing.

Indicated hypothesis is not a recognized term within the framework of hypothesis testing in statistics.
