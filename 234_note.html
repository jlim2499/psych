Stats (key epidemiology terms)


An epidemic (aka an outbreak) is an increase above the expected level over a given time period of disease in a defined population.

An endemic is the persistent, usual, or expected level of disease in a given population.

A pandemic involves epidemics affecting a large number of people across many countries, continents or regions.
