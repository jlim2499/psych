387.The median value
71 The find the median and the quartiles, first order the values

62, 63, 64, 64, 70, 72, 76, 77, 81, 81

The median is the middle vale (that has 50% of values above and below).

In this case the middle value is between the 5th and 6th value so is (70+72)/2 which equals 71.


388.The first quartile
64 The first quartile is basically the median of the lower half of values. The lower half is those values below the median.

The lower half consists of

62, 63, 64, 64, 70

The median of this group (Q1) is 64


389.The interquartile range
13 The IQR (interqualie range) is obtained by subtracting Q3 from Q1

Q3 is obtained by finding the median of the upper half.

The upper half includes the following values

72, 76, 77, 81, 81

The median of the upper half (Q3) is 77.

So

IQR = Q3 - Q1
IQR = 77 - 64
IQR = 13
