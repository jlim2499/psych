The Delphi method

The correct answer is The Delphi method. The Delphi method is a structured communication technique, originally developed as a systematic, interactive forecasting method which relies on a panel of experts. In the provided abstract, the process described aligns with this method: an initial survey is conducted (round one), followed by iterative rounds where items are re-rated based on consensus reached in previous rounds. This process continues until a predefined level of consensus is achieved. As stated in the abstract, 'Items rated by at least 80% of experts as essential or important were included.' This indicates that the Delphi method was used.
