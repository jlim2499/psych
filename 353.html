How would the association of the variables seen in the scatter graph be best described?



Non-linear correlation

The correct answer is Non-linear correlation. The scatter plot displays a distinct curved pattern, suggesting a non-linear relationship between the variables. The points are not randomly scattered nor do they form a straight line, which indicates that the relationship is not linear.
